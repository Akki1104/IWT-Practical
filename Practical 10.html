WELCOME TO GOVERNMENT POLYTECHNIC,NARENDRA NAGAR(T.G)

Government Polytechnic, Narendranagar was established by the Government of Uttar Pradesh in 1975, for imparting technical education and to generate suitable technical manpower. The institute is fully financed by Government of Uttarakhand.
It offers Diploma in Engineering in six branches, namely Electrical Engineering, Electronics Engineering ,Information Technology,Civil Engineering,Mechanical Engineering,Gaming&Animation and Diploma in Pharmacy and P.G. Diploma in Computer Application .
Message
Government Polytechnic, Narendranagar is an institution that aims the complete development of the students. The Institute has been offering diploma in various disciplines. Institute has a glorious history of thirty seven years in imparting quality Engineering Education .

Ever since its establishment our Experienced and highly qualified faculty has been trying to deliver its best in all the spheres of a student's all round development, whether its education , extra-curricular , sports and much more.
